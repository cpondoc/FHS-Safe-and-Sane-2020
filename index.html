Frederick High School Safe and Sane 2020

Welcome to the official site for the Frederick High School Safe and Sane 2020!

What is Safe and Sane?

Safe and Sane is a party held at Adventure Park to celebrate the seniors graduating the night they graduate. See some of the great activities you get to partake in below!


West World Laser Tag!

This multi-level western town is sure to keep you and your friends coming back to face the Wild Wild West again and again.

Hologate Virtual Reality!

When you put on the HTC Vive headset, you will be immediately transported to a new world; a new reality; a new dimension of thrill.

Wild West Express!

The Wild West Express Roller Coaster soars higher than Adventure Park USA’s other coaster, the Wildcat, at 53 feet tall, featuring tight turns and heart-pounding drops.



Mini Golf!

Adventure Park USA's challenging 18-hole miniature golf coursesprovide excitement for the whole family as you make your way over rampant river beds, between looming spires, and across engulfing canyons.

Blazing Trail Go Karts!

Race around the Family Track in our Formula Karts or our double karts, or take the younger ones to drive the Rookie Karts.

Crater Lake Bumper Boats!

Nothing is better than sailing around Adventure Park USA’s Crater Lake in a Shockwave Bumper Boat on a hot summer day.



General Details

Below are some of the genreral details one must know to be a part of Safe and Sane.



  • When?


    Frederick High Safe and Sane will be held on June 1, 2020. The graduates will arrive at 10 p.m. aboard a FCPS bus and will be “locked in” until the bus takes them back to FHS at 5 a.m.


  • Where?


    Frederick High Safe and Sane will be held at Adventure Park, which is located at: 11113 Baldwin Rd, Monrovia, MD 21770. Directions from Frederick High School are shown to the right.



  • For Whom?


    Frederick High Safe and Sane is open only to the graduating class of seniors, also known as the class of 2020.
